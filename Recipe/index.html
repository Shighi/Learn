Bean Stew and Chapati Recipe

Ingredients

Bean Stew

  • 1 bunch spring onion
  • 3 medium sized tomatoes
  • 1 medium sized carrot
  • 1 green capsicum (hoho)
  • 1 Bunch dhania
  • 1 courgette
  • 2 royco cubes
  • 1/2 tablespoon salt
  • cooking oil
  • 1/4 kg beans

Chapati

  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 tablespoon salt
  • 1 carrot (grated)
  • cooking oil

Instructions

  1. Assemble all ingredients for the bean stew.
  2. In a sufuria, pour some cooking oil and add your onions. Cook to your liking, then add the carrots and cover for a minute.
  3. Add your diced hoho, courgettes, sprinkle the royco cubes and salt; let it cook for a few minutes till the courgettes become soft.
  4. Add tomatoes and cover; wait for a few minutes and stir.
  5. Add your beans, put some little water and boil till the soup thickens.
  6. Finally, add some dhania, stir, and remove from heat.
  7. Knead your dough to a soft one with all your ingredients.
  8. Divide your dough into equal portions.
  9. Dust one ball with flour and roll to a round shape.
  10. Heat the pan till hot and fry till golden brown.
